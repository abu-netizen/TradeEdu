Questions

Frequently asked questions.

Is TradeEdu financial advice?

No. TradeEdu provides education only. It does not provide financial advice, investment recommendations, trading signals, or guaranteed returns.

Can beginners join?

Yes. The beginner program is designed for people who need to learn trading language, market basics, risk awareness, and simple chart concepts.

Do you teach live trading?

The focus is education, practice routines, and structured learning. Any live-market examples are for educational discussion only, not instructions to trade.

Why do you ask for a phone number?

The phone number helps confirm serious inquiries and allows a program advisor to contact learners about education options. The form checks for complete phone formatting, but real phone verification requires SMS OTP integration.
